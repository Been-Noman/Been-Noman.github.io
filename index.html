Current Research

Simulated Quantum Annealing–Genetic Algorithm Framework for Nuclear Fuel Loading Optimization

  • Developed a three-layer hybrid framework integrating quantum-inspired metaheuristics, deep-learning surrogate modeling, and evolutionary search for PWR fuel loading optimization.
  • Formulated multi-constraint fuel reloading placement as a 579-variable QUBO problem using PyQUBO and Simulated Quantum Annealing.
  • Trained a multi-output residual CNN surrogate with positional encoding and squeeze-and-excitation attention on 10,000 OpenMC Monte Carlo simulations.
  • Accelerated GA-based fitness evaluation from approximately 12 hours to under a second while targeting reduced peak power factors.

Current Research

Quantum-Mechanical Study of the Electronic Properties of UxPuyOz Compounds

  • Conducted first-principles DFT simulations using Quantum ESPRESSO for uranium-based mixed oxides.
  • Calculated and benchmarked total energy, band gap, DOS/PDOS, stress, and force parameters.
  • Studied structure–property relationships to identify materials with promising mechanical and electronic performance.
  • Compared simulation outcomes with literature data for validation and benchmarking.

Selected Projects

Quantum Computing

Quantum Galton Board

Built Qiskit-based quantum circuits for normal, exponential, and Hadamard walk distributions inspired by the Universal Statistical Simulator framework. The implementation uses Pauli-X initialization, amplitude control, CSWAP routing, ancilla-assisted diffusion, noisy backend modeling, and transpilation optimization.

Reported high fidelities for smaller circuits and robust distribution alignment under IBM FakeGeneva noise models.
